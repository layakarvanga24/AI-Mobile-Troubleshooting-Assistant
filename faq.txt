 #### Section 1: Plans & Billing (Extended)

Q: How can I check my prepaid balance?
A: Dial *121#, use the provider app, or send SMS “BAL” to 12345.

Q: How do I pay my postpaid bill?
A: Use UPI, credit/debit card, wallet, or net banking via the app or website.

Q: Can I enable auto-pay for postpaid bills?
A: Yes. Go to App > Billing > AutoPay and link a debit/credit card or UPI AutoPay.

Q: What happens if I pay late?
A: After 3 days a late fee applies. After 10 days services may be suspended until cleared.

Q: Can I switch from prepaid to postpaid?
A: Yes. Submit KYC in-store or in-app. Activation is usually within 24–48 hours.

Q: Can I downgrade my postpaid plan?
A: Yes. Plan downgrade is allowed once per billing cycle via the app or customer support.

Q: What is the minimum recharge for prepaid to stay active?
A: A minimum recharge of ₹99 (or local equivalent) every 28 days is required to keep outgoing services active.

Q: What happens if I don’t recharge?
A: Outgoing services are suspended after grace period. Incoming calls may last 7–15 more days before deactivation.

Q: Can I share data with my family?
A: Yes. Family Sharing plans allow up to 5 members. Set this up in App > Family > Data Sharing.

Q: How do I get an itemized bill statement?
A: Download from App > Billing > Past Bills or request a detailed PDF via email.

Q: How do I activate an unlimited night data plan?
A: Recharge with a night-data add-on pack. Usage usually applies from 12 AM–6 AM only.

Q: Is there a weekend rollover benefit?
A: Some postpaid plans carry unused weekday data into weekends. Check plan details in the app.

Q: Can I carry forward unused data?
A: Yes, postpaid plans often allow rollover up to 200GB. Prepaid does not usually include rollover.

Q: Do prepaid packs include OTT subscriptions?
A: Yes, some recharge packs include 30–90 days of OTT apps like Netflix, Amazon Prime, or Disney+ Hotstar.

Q: Can I pause my postpaid plan while traveling abroad?
A: Some providers allow “Seasonal Suspension” for 1–3 months with a nominal fee.

Q: How do I activate international roaming?
A: App > Roaming > Activate. Prepaid requires minimum balance, postpaid may add a refundable deposit.

Q: Can I recharge international roaming for just a week?
A: Yes. Providers offer 7-day and 14-day roaming packs.

Q: How do I check my bill due date?
A: App > Billing > Current Bill. SMS “BILL” to 12345 also shows the due date.

Q: How do I upgrade my prepaid pack mid-cycle?
A: Recharge with a higher-value pack. New validity overrides current pack.

Q: What are family postpaid plans?
A: Shared billing where 2–5 numbers are grouped under one master account with pooled data.

Q: Can I gift data to a friend?
A: Yes. Use App > Services > Data Gift. Minimum 500MB transferable.

Q: Are there student discount plans?
A: Yes. Student SIM plans include 20% extra data and OTT bundles. Valid student ID is required.

Q: How do I activate a corporate plan?
A: Corporate HR must register numbers under enterprise billing. Employees get discounted rates.

Q: Can I pay my bill in installments?
A: Some providers allow EMI for bills above ₹2000 using credit cards.

Q: How do I know if I’m eligible for a 5G upgrade?
A: Log in to the app > Plans. Eligible numbers will see a “5G Ready” badge.

Q: Can I recharge someone else’s number?
A: Yes. Enter their number in the app or website recharge portal and pay.

Q: What is bill cycle date change?
A: Postpaid customers can request to shift billing date once every 6 months.

Q: Can I combine multiple services (broadband, DTH, mobile) under one bill?
A: Yes. Providers offer “One Bill” bundles with discounts up to 15%.

Q: How do I activate emergency loan talktime?
A: Dial *150# or use App > Emergency Credit. The amount will be deducted in the next recharge.

Q: Can I set data usage limits for my plan?
A: Yes. In App > Data Settings > Usage Limit. After the limit, data speed reduces automatically.

Q: How do I check offers available on my number?
A: Dial *121# > My Offers or check in the app under Exclusive Offers.

Q: Are there senior citizen special plans?
A: Yes. Some providers offer discounted calling packs and priority customer care for 60+ customers.

Q: What is a top-up recharge?
A: Extra talktime-only recharge without changing plan validity.

Q: How do I activate international calling (ISD)?
A: Recharge with an ISD pack or enable ISD by depositing security amount for postpaid.

Q: Can I link multiple numbers to one payment method?
A: Yes. Save your card/UPI in the app and select multiple linked accounts for auto-pay.

Q: What is bill shock protection?
A: A safeguard where postpaid bills are capped at a preset amount. Services pause once the cap is hit.

Q: How do I get a GST invoice for my bill?
A: Download from App > Billing > Invoice. Registered GST number must be updated in profile.

Q: Can I split my postpaid bill with roommates?
A: Officially no, but you can use UPI AutoPay to auto-charge multiple accounts after bill generation.

Q: Are unlimited calling plans truly unlimited?
A: Yes, within fair usage policies (e.g., 3000 minutes/month). Beyond this, per-minute charges apply.

Q: How do I activate a yearly prepaid plan?
A: Recharge with an annual pack via app, website, or retailer. Validity is usually 365 days.

Q: Can I schedule auto-recharge for prepaid?
A: Yes, in the app > Recharge > Auto-Schedule. You can set monthly or weekly auto-recharges.

Q: How do I link my credit card for rewards billing?
A: App > Billing > Payment Options > Link Card. Reward points are credited each cycle.

Q: Why is my prepaid recharge pending?
A: Sometimes due to bank delays. Wait 15 minutes. If not resolved, raise a support ticket.

Q: Can I cancel a wrong recharge?
A: No, prepaid recharges are non-refundable once processed. Postpaid bill payments can be reversed within 24 hours if double paid.

Q: What is a combo plan?
A: A recharge that includes talktime, data, and SMS benefits bundled into one pack.

Q: How do I activate unlimited calling for ISD?
A: Providers offer ISD calling packs. Recharge with an ISD add-on and enable in call settings.

Q: Why is my bill amount higher than plan cost?
A: Additional usage, value-added services, roaming, taxes, or late fees may be added.

Q: Can I remove VAS (Value-Added Services) charges from my bill?
A: Yes. Go to App > Services > Manage Subscriptions and deactivate unwanted services.

Q: How do I get a refund for a failed bill payment?
A: Refunds are usually processed within 3–7 working days to the original payment method.

Q: Can I keep my number if I change plans?
A: Yes. Mobile number portability applies within and across operators.

Q: How do I activate bill alerts?
A: Enable SMS/email notifications in App > Profile > Notifications > Billing Alerts.

Q: Can I use two prepaid packs at the same time?
A: Yes. The latest recharge usually overrides the older one, but some packs can run in parallel (like data add-ons).

Q: What is a data add-on pack?
A: Extra data recharge that works alongside your base plan validity.

Q: Can I use international roaming on prepaid without a pack?
A: Technically yes, but charges are extremely high. Packs are strongly recommended.

Q: How do I check my credit limit in postpaid?
A: App > Billing > Credit Limit. You’ll also receive alerts when nearing the limit.

Q: What is fair usage policy (FUP)?
A: A limit on “unlimited” benefits (calls/data). After crossing FUP, usage may slow down or extra charges apply.

Q: Can I pay my bill in foreign currency?
A: No. Bills must be paid in local currency. Use an international card; conversion will happen automatically.

Q: Why is my prepaid pack validity showing 27 days instead of 28?
A: Some packs count calendar days (billing cycle aligned) instead of fixed days. Check plan terms.

Q: Can I transfer prepaid balance to another number?
A: Some operators allow balance transfer via USSD or app. Usually up to ₹50 per day.

Q: What is OTT bundle recharge?
A: A special recharge that includes data + free subscription to Netflix/Prime/Hotstar.

Q: How do I activate international SMS service?
A: Recharge with an ISD SMS pack or enable ISD by security deposit (postpaid).

Q: Can I combine prepaid data with Wi-Fi broadband plan?
A: Yes, in “Converged Plans” where mobile + broadband share data quota.

Q: What happens if my bill is disputed?
A: Raise a complaint in the app or helpline. Disputed amounts are withheld until investigation is complete.

Q: Can I pay my bill using cryptocurrency?
A: Currently no, most providers accept only INR/USD equivalents via banking channels.

Q: Are GST charges included in my plan?
A: No, taxes (e.g., 18% GST in India) are added on top of base plan price for postpaid.

Q: Can I port my number while having unpaid bills?
A: No. Clear all outstanding dues before porting.

Q: What is an unlimited prepaid plan?
A: A pack that includes daily data (e.g., 2GB/day), unlimited calls, and 100 SMS/day, valid for 28/56/84 days.

Q: Why was my auto-pay declined?
A: Insufficient bank balance, expired card, or exceeded UPI mandate limit.

Q: Can I pause auto-pay temporarily?
A: Yes, App > Billing > AutoPay > Pause. You can resume anytime.



 #### Section 2: Devices & Warranty
Q: What is the standard warranty for devices?
A: Most devices have 12 months for handset, 6 months for battery and charger.

Q: Is accidental damage covered?
A: Only if you buy Care+ within 7 days of activation.

Q: How do I claim warranty?
A: Visit an authorized service center with invoice and IMEI proof.

Q: Can I buy extended warranty?
A: Yes, extended warranty up to 2 years is available for most models.

Q: How do I check my phone’s IMEI?
A: Dial *#06# or go to Settings > About Phone > IMEI.

Q: What accessories are covered in warranty?
A: Only bundled charger and cable. Earphones may vary by region.

Q: What does the standard device warranty cover?
A: It usually covers manufacturing defects in hardware for 12 months and accessories for 6 months.

Q: Are screen damages covered under warranty?
A: No, physical damages such as broken screens are not covered unless you purchased screen protection insurance.

Q: Can I extend my device warranty?
A: Yes. Many brands offer extended warranty plans for 1–2 years at an additional cost.

Q: How do I check my device warranty status?
A: Dial *#06# to get the IMEI and check warranty on the manufacturer’s official website or app.

Q: Is water damage covered under warranty?
A: No. Even water-resistant phones are not covered for liquid damage in standard warranty policies.

Q: Can I replace my device if it overheats frequently?
A: If diagnosed as a manufacturing defect by an authorized service center, replacement is possible.

Q: What is DOA (Dead on Arrival) policy?
A: If your device fails within 7 days of purchase, most brands offer a free replacement.

Q: How do I claim warranty for accessories like chargers?
A: Submit the accessory along with the purchase invoice at an authorized service center.

Q: Can I claim warranty without a bill?
A: Some brands allow claims with IMEI-based verification, but ideally keep the purchase invoice.

Q: What is covered under extended warranty?
A: It typically extends the same coverage as standard warranty—hardware and manufacturing defects only.

Q: Are scratches and dents covered in warranty?
A: No, cosmetic damages are excluded.

Q: Can refurbished phones have warranty?
A: Yes, but the warranty period is usually shorter (3–6 months).

Q: Do headphones and Bluetooth devices have warranty?
A: Yes, usually 6–12 months, depending on the brand.

Q: How do I claim international warranty?
A: Some brands offer global warranty. You can walk into an authorized service center abroad with invoice + IMEI.

Q: Is battery replacement covered under warranty?
A: Yes, if the battery drains unusually fast due to manufacturing defect, not normal wear.

Q: Can I transfer my warranty if I sell my phone?
A: Yes, warranty is tied to the device IMEI, not the owner.

Q: What happens if the service center cannot repair my device?
A: You may receive a replacement or a credit note based on company policy.

Q: Are chargers and cables included in warranty claims?
A: Yes, but usually only for 6 months.

Q: Can I purchase insurance for accidental damages?
A: Yes. Many providers sell add-on insurance for drops, liquid damage, and theft.

Q: Does rooting or jailbreaking void warranty?
A: Yes, unauthorized modifications void standard warranty immediately.

Q: Can I claim warranty if I lose the box?
A: Yes, the box is not required. Invoice and IMEI are sufficient.

Q: How do I book a service appointment online?
A: Use the brand’s official service app or website to schedule appointments.

Q: Can I get doorstep repair service?
A: Some brands offer doorstep pick-up and drop repair service.

Q: How long does a warranty repair usually take?
A: Typically 5–7 business days, depending on part availability.

Q: What is out-of-warranty service?
A: Repairs done after warranty expiry. Charges are applicable for parts and labor.

Q: Can I extend warranty after expiry?
A: No, warranty must be extended before the original warranty expires.

Q: What is covered in accidental protection plans?
A: Drops, liquid damage, cracked screens, and hardware failures due to accidents.

Q: Do third-party repairs affect warranty?
A: Yes, if you repair your phone at unauthorized shops, official warranty becomes void.

Q: Can I get a temporary replacement while my phone is in service?
A: Some brands offer loaner devices, but not all.

Q: How do I track my repair status?
A: Use service center ticket number on the official repair tracking portal.

Q: Can I replace a faulty screen protector under warranty?
A: No, screen protectors and cases are not covered under warranty.

Q: Is data recovery included in warranty service?
A: No, users are advised to back up data before giving devices for service.

Q: Can I claim warranty if the device is imported?
A: Only if the device supports international warranty. Grey market imports may not be eligible.

Q: How many times can I claim warranty?
A: Unlimited, within the warranty period, as long as the issue is genuine.

Q: Do I need to register my device for warranty?
A: Some brands auto-register via IMEI, while others require registration through apps.

Q: Are wireless earbuds covered in device warranty?
A: Yes, but usually separately under accessories coverage.

Q: Can I claim warranty if I flashed a custom ROM?
A: No, modifying system software voids warranty.

Q: What happens if my device cannot be repaired and is out of warranty?
A: You will be offered a paid replacement at a discounted price.

Q: Do cracked back panels qualify for warranty?

Q: Are foldable screens covered in warranty?
A: Yes, but brands may apply special terms due to higher fragility.

Q: Are refurbished phones eligible for the same warranty as new phones?
A: No, refurbished phones usually have a shorter warranty period, like 3 to 6 months.

Q: What happens if my device fails after warranty expiration?

Q: Can a cracked camera lens be covered under warranty?

Q: Is a swollen battery covered under warranty?

Q: Can I buy extended warranty after 6 months of purchase?

Q: Does warranty cover overheating issues?

Q: Can I replace my device charger for free if it stops working?

Q: Is accidental liquid spillage on the device covered in extended warranty?

Q: Do wireless chargers have warranty?

 #### Section 3: Troubleshooting - Network

Q: My phone shows “No Service”. What should I do?
A: Restart phone, reseat SIM, try manual network selection, or reset APN.

Q: Calls drop frequently. Why?
A: Could be weak signal. Try enabling Wi-Fi calling or reseating SIM.

Q: Data speed is slow on 5G. How to fix?
A: Ensure 5G is enabled in settings, toggle airplane mode, and check coverage map.

Q: My SIM is not recognized.
A: Clean SIM tray, insert correctly, or try in another phone.

Q: How do I enable VoLTE?
A: Settings > Mobile Network > Enable VoLTE.

Q: My phone shows “Emergency Calls Only”. What should I do?  
A: Manually select your carrier under Settings > Mobile Networks > Network Operators.  

Q: Why does my phone say “SIM Not Provisioned”?  
A: Your SIM may not be activated yet. Contact your carrier or try reinserting the SIM.  

Q: My eSIM is not activating. What can I try?  
A: Delete the eSIM profile and re-scan the QR code from your carrier’s app.  

Q: I cannot send SMS messages.  
A: Verify SMSC number, ensure mobile data is enabled for MMS, and clear the messaging app cache.  

Q: Incoming calls are failing.  
A: Check Do Not Disturb settings, confirm SIM is active, and test with another device.  

Q: Outgoing calls disconnect instantly.  
A: Disable VoLTE temporarily and retry, then update carrier settings if issue persists.  

Q: My data works on Wi-Fi but not on mobile.  
A: Reset APN settings to default and check if your plan includes mobile data.  

Q: I have full bars but no internet. Why?  
A: Switch to a different network mode (4G instead of 5G) and toggle airplane mode.  

Q: Roaming is not working abroad.  
A: Enable Data Roaming, restart your device, and manually connect to a local network.  

Q: Why is hotspot not working?  
A: Check if your plan supports tethering, reset network settings, and reconfigure hotspot security.  

Q: Wi-Fi Calling doesn’t work.  
A: Enable Wi-Fi Calling in Settings > Phone > Wi-Fi Calling and ensure a strong Wi-Fi connection.  

Q: My phone keeps switching between Wi-Fi and mobile data.  
A: Disable “Switch to Mobile Data” under Wi-Fi advanced settings.  

Q: Network drops while driving.  
A: Set Preferred Network Mode to Auto and update carrier settings for better handovers.  

Q: Why is my SIM showing inactive?  
A: Toggle airplane mode, reset network settings, or request a new SIM if old.  

Q: Can’t connect to enterprise Wi-Fi.  
A: Make sure correct security (WPA2-Enterprise) and certificate are installed.  

Q: Mobile data works only on SIM1, not SIM2.  
A: Check SIM Manager > Preferred SIM for Data and swap SIMs between slots.  

Q: Calls fail when switching from Wi-Fi to mobile.  
A: Disable Wi-Fi Calling or update carrier settings for smoother handover.  

Q: Why is MMS not sending?  
A: Ensure mobile data is on and APN includes “mms” type.  

Q: My 5G icon is missing.  
A: Confirm 5G coverage, update SIM, and enable 5G under Preferred Network Type.  

Q: Call quality is poor.  
A: Enable VoLTE or Wi-Fi calling, and ensure microphone isn’t obstructed.  

Q: Why do I hear echo during calls?  
A: Reduce call volume, disable speakerphone, and test with another headset.  

Q: Internet works but video calls keep dropping.  
A: Lower video quality in the app and ensure background downloads are paused.  

Q: My VPN won’t connect over mobile data.  
A: Check APN settings and disable battery saver, which may block VPN tunnels.  

Q: Why is my phone stuck on 3G?  
A: Change Preferred Network Mode to 4G/5G and restart your device.  

Q: Network shows different carrier name.  
A: Restart the phone and manually reselect the correct operator.  

Q: SIM tray is damaged, what happens?  
A: Visit a service center for replacement; improper tray alignment may prevent SIM detection.  

Q: Why do I get high roaming charges?  
A: Track roaming usage under Settings > Data Usage and buy a roaming pack in advance.  

Q: My Wi-Fi is connected but internet doesn’t work.  
A: Try changing DNS to 8.8.8.8 (Google) or 1.1.1.1 (Cloudflare).  

Q: My SIM toolkit app is not working.  
A: Clear cache of SIM Toolkit app and restart device.  

Q: Can’t enable hotspot after update.  
A: Reset network settings and check carrier policy for tethering restrictions.  

Q: Calls don’t connect in 5G mode.  
A: Switch to 4G and retry, as some networks don’t yet support VoNR (Voice over 5G).  

Q: Why does Wi-Fi keep turning off?  
A: Disable Wi-Fi Power Saving and Smart Wi-Fi features in settings.  

Q: Why does my phone say “Invalid SIM”?  
A: The SIM may be damaged, expired, or locked to another carrier. Try another SIM card or request a replacement.  

Q: My phone is stuck in “Searching for Network”.  
A: Reset network settings, reinsert SIM, and update to the latest software version.  

Q: I can’t receive OTP SMS messages.  
A: Clear the SMS app cache, disable spam filters, and ensure SMS Center settings are correct.  

Q: Why is VoWiFi not available on my phone?  
A: Ensure carrier supports Wi-Fi calling on your device model and enable VoWiFi in settings.  

Q: My phone cannot connect to 4G LTE after update.  
A: Delete old APN profiles, re-add the correct one, and restart the device.  

Q: Incoming international calls don’t reach me.  
A: Verify that call barring is off and international roaming is enabled on your plan.  

Q: Why is my mobile data usage unusually high?  
A: Check background apps, disable auto-updates, and enable Data Saver.  

Q: Mobile network disconnects when using Bluetooth headset.  
A: Turn off VoLTE temporarily and check for interference with Bluetooth.  

Q: SIM card PIN keeps locking.  
A: Disable SIM lock under Settings > Security > SIM Card Lock, or reset via PUK code.  

Q: I hear “Number not in service” even for valid numbers.  
A: Update carrier settings and ensure correct dialing format with country code.  

Q: My device doesn’t support carrier aggregation.  
A: Some phones lack hardware support. Confirm specs or request firmware update if supported.  

Q: Why do I get constant “Roaming indicator on”?  
A: Disable “National Roaming” if you’re in your home country and reset APN.  

Q: Data works but I cannot use WhatsApp.  
A: Clear app cache, re-enable background data, and check if carrier is blocking VoIP services.  

Q: Why do I get random “Network registration failed” messages?  
A: Could be a SIM or antenna issue. Test SIM in another phone.  

Q: Mobile data disconnects when screen turns off.  
A: Disable battery optimization for the network service or messaging apps.  

Q: Why can’t I switch between 2G/3G/4G networks?  
A: Some carriers lock network mode. Check Preferred Network in SIM settings.  

Q: I cannot connect to VPN over mobile data.  
A: Enable IPsec/L2TP protocols and check if carrier restricts VPN.  

Q: Carrier settings update keeps popping up.  
A: Install the update; if it repeats, reset network settings.  

Q: Why is VoLTE missing after SIM swap?  
A: Contact carrier to enable VoLTE on new SIM and update phone software.  

Q: Phone shows two networks at once on dual SIM. Is it normal?  
A: Yes, dual SIM devices maintain standby on both, but only one SIM can use data at a time.  

Q: Why is my number showing as “Unknown” in settings?  
A: Edit SIM card number manually under Settings > SIM Manager > SIM Info.  

Q: I cannot receive calls when data is active.  
A: Disable Data-only mode and enable VoLTE to allow simultaneous call and data.  

Q: My APN resets after every reboot.  
A: Delete duplicate APNs and keep only the one provided by your carrier.  

Q: Why does my hotspot show “Connected, no internet”?  
A: Recreate hotspot with WPA2 security, reset network, and verify plan supports tethering.  

Q: Signal strength drops when holding phone.  
A: This is antenna attenuation. Use a case or avoid covering antenna bands.  

Q: Phone shows “IMS Registration Failed”.  
A: Reset IMS settings, enable VoLTE, and check with your carrier support.  

Q: Why do I get “Call ended” immediately after dialing?  
A: Could be SIM restriction or call barring. Disable barring under Call Settings.  

Q: My device connects to 4G but speed is slower than 3G.  
A: Congestion could be the cause. Switch bands using Network Mode if available.  

Q: Why is carrier aggregation disabled in my area?  
A: Not all towers support it; contact your operator for availability.  

Q: SIM doesn’t work after phone software update.  
A: Perform a factory reset or downgrade if issue persists.  
 

 #### Section 4: Battery & Performance

Q: My phone battery drains quickly.
A: Reduce brightness, use battery saver, and restrict background apps.

Q: How do I improve gaming performance?
A: Enable Game Mode, close background apps, and reduce resolution.

Q: Is overnight charging harmful?
A: Modern phones manage charging automatically, but avoid keeping plugged in for days.

Q: What charger should I use?
A: Always use certified chargers (25W/45W PD recommended for fast charging).

Q: How do I enable power saving?
A: Settings > Battery > Power Saving Mode.

Q: Why does my phone get hot while charging?  
A: Charging generates heat; use the original charger, avoid heavy usage during charging, and keep the phone on a cool surface.  

Q: My phone lags after software update. What can I do?  
A: Clear cache partition, update all apps, and consider a factory reset if issue persists.  

Q: How do I check battery health?  
A: Go to Settings > Battery > Battery Health (on some phones) or use diagnostic apps.  

Q: Does fast charging reduce battery lifespan?  
A: Minimal impact in modern devices, but frequent use of regular (slower) charging can extend lifespan.  

Q: Why does my phone randomly restart?
